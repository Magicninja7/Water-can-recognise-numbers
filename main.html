How the Algorithm Works

This algorithm analyzes the drawing on the canvas using water to detect and recognize numbers. By spilling it over the number, you see how much is left inside, giving you a parameter. Slice each half into two, and you've got 8 params. The 9th paramater is how much water is displaced, if the number were to be dropped into a pool of water.

Although you cannot visualise 9 dimensions, even using a 2d graph, you can still see where the vectors of each number are grouped.

Next you find n'th nearest neighbours of your numbers vector, and see which number is most common; this is your guess.
